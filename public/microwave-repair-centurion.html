Professional Microwave Oven Repair

A faulty microwave can disrupt your kitchen routine. Whether it's not heating, sparking, making strange noises, or the door won't close properly — MyFixer provides fast, reliable, and professional microwave repair services.

Our certified technicians diagnose problems accurately and fix them the first time. We service all types — countertop, built-in, over-the-range, and commercial microwaves from all major brands.

Common Microwave Problems We Repair

  • Not heating / no power to magnetron
  • Sparking / arcing inside
  • Turntable not rotating
  • Door switch or latch problems
  • Strange noises / buzzing
  • Control panel / keypad issues
  • Light or fan not working
  • Display error codes / blank screen
