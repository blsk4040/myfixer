Expert Refrigerator Repair

Whether your fridge has stopped cooling, your freezer is icing up, making loud noises, or keeps tripping the power — MyFixer provides fast, reliable and affordable repairs you can trust. Our qualified technicians diagnose faults properly and repair them correctly the first time.

We repair single-door fridges, double-door fridges, side-by-side units, chest freezers, upright freezers and commercial refrigeration. All major brands including Bosch, Samsung, LG, Defy, Hisense, KIC, Whirlpool, Siemens, AEG, SMEG, Kelvinator and more.

Fridge & Freezer Problems We Fix

  • Fridge not cooling or getting warm
  • Freezer not freezing properly
  • Excessive frost or ice build-up
  • Gas leak detection & gas refills (R600a / R134a)
  • Compressor repair & replacement
  • Loud humming or clicking noises
  • Water leaking inside or underneath
  • Fridge keeps tripping electricity
