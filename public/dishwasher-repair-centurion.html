Professional Dishwasher Repair

A faulty dishwasher can be frustrating and inconvenient — whether it’s not draining, leaking water everywhere, not washing dishes properly or refusing to switch on. MyFixer offers reliable, affordable and expert dishwasher repair services you can trust.

Our qualified technicians diagnose faults correctly and repair them properly the first time. We repair built-in, freestanding and compact dishwashers for homes, offices, restaurants and commercial users. All makes, all models.

Common Dishwasher Problems We Repair

  • Dishwasher not draining / water remains inside
  • Dishwasher leaking water from door or underneath
  • Not washing / dishes still dirty after cycle
  • Dishwasher not switching on / power faults
  • Not heating water / cold wash only
  • Dishwasher stuck mid-cycle
  • Bad smells or standing dirty water
  • Spray arms not rotating
  • Error codes / beeping
  • Loud noise, grinding or rattling
