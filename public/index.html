myfixer is your trusted local expert for fast, reliable appliance repair and installation across Centurion, Pretoria, and Johannesburg.

Our team of certified technicians brings decades of combined experience, expertly handling fridges, washing machines, TVs, ovens, dishwashers, coffee machines, HVAC systems, and more – all major brands covered with genuine parts.
