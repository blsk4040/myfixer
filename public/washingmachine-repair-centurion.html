Professional Washing Machine Repair

A malfunctioning washing machine can disrupt your household. Whether it’s not spinning, leaking, draining poorly, or refusing to turn on — MyFixer provides fast, reliable, and professional washing machine repair services.

Our certified technicians accurately diagnose problems and fix them the first time. We service front-load, top-load, and compact washing machines from all major brands.

Common Washing Machine Problems We Repair

  • Not spinning / drum stuck
  • Water leakage / pipe or door seal issues
  • Drainage problems / faulty pump
  • Electrical / power faults
  • Strange noises during cycles
  • Error codes on display panel
  • Not filling with water
  • Overflows or overflowing cycles
  • Smart washing machine software faults
  • Uneven washing / vibrations
