Fridge Acting Funny? Don’t Let It Spoil Your Day!

Imagine opening your fridge to find warm milk and wilted veggies – nightmare! Here’s how to spot and fix common fridge woes before they escalate in your Pretoria or Centurion home.

Top 5 Signs Your Fridge Needs Attention

Not Cooling Enough

Food spoiling fast or warm spots? Often caused by dirty coils or blocked vents. Clean them first — if it still doesn’t cool, low refrigerant or a failing compressor could be the culprit.

Frost Overload

Freezer looking like the Arctic? The defrost timer or heater is probably busted. A manual defrost helps temporarily, but call us to stop it happening again.

Leaky Mess

Puddles on the floor? Usually a clogged drain line. Try clearing it with hot water — persistent leaks mean the valve or pipe needs replacement.

Weird Sounds

Buzzing, rattling or grinding? The fan or compressor is struggling. Ignore it and you risk a total breakdown.

Always Running

High electricity bill and the fridge never switches off? Bad door seals are the usual suspect. Test with a Rand note — if it pulls out easily, the seal needs replacing.

Pro Tip: Clean the coils behind or underneath your fridge every month — it can save you hundreds on repairs and electricity!

Washing Machine Woes: From Leaks to Loud Noises

Laundry day disaster? We've all been there – soggy floors and half-washed clothes. Let's break down why your washer is rebelling and how to tame it in Centurion.

Key Warning Signs & Quick Fixes

No Spin Cycle

Clothes dripping wet? Overload or lid switch fault. Balance load; if no luck, sensor needs replacing.

Leaking Everywhere

Flooded laundry room? Check hoses for cracks. Tighten connections – pro for internal leaks.

Shaking Violently

Machine dancing? Uneven floor or worn shocks. Level it; replace parts if ongoing.

Won't Start

Power on but no action? Door lock issue. Test outlet; control board might be fried.

Smelly Drum

Musty clothes? Run hot cycle with vinegar. Persistent? Deep clean or mold removal needed.

Fun Fact: Overloading causes 40% of breakdowns! Keep it light for longer life.

TV Troubles: Fix That Fuzzy Screen Before Movie Night

Nothing kills binge-watching vibe like a glitchy TV. From blackouts to bizarre lines – here's your guide to reviving your entertainment hub in Johannesburg.

Must-Know TV Issues

Blank Screen (Sound OK)

Hear but can't see? Backlight gone. Power cycle; pro for LED replacement.

Coloured Lines

Streaks across screen? Panel damage. Avoid pressure; might need new display.

Flickering Madness

Image unstable? HDMI loose or inverter fault. Swap cables first.

Silent Treatment

Picture but no audio? Speaker blown. Check settings; repair amp.

No Power

Dead set? Fuse or board issue. Unplug 5 mins; call if no luck.

Pro Tip: Dust vents regularly to prevent overheating and extend TV life.

AC Breaking a Sweat? Beat the Heat with These Tips

Gauteng summers are brutal – don't let a faulty aircon turn your home into a sauna. Spot these signs early for cool relief.

AC Alert Signals

Blowing Hot Air

Not cooling? Low freon or dirty filters. Change filter monthly.

Ice on Unit

Frozen coils? Airflow blocked. Thaw and clean; check refrigerant.

Water Leaks

Indoor puddles? Drain line clogged. Vinegar flush as temp fix.

Weak Breeze

Poor flow? Vents dirty or fan failing. Clean grills.

Noisy Operation

Rattling? Loose parts. Tighten; pro for compressor.

Maintenance Hack: Annual servicing saves 15% on bills and prevents breakdowns.
