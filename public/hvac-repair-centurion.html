Professional HVAC & Air Conditioner Repair

A broken HVAC or air conditioner can disrupt your comfort at home or work. Whether it’s not cooling, leaking, making strange noises, or refusing to turn on — MyFixer provides fast, reliable, and professional HVAC repair services.

Our certified technicians accurately diagnose problems and fix them the first time. We service split ACs, ducted systems, window units, and commercial HVAC systems from all major brands.

Common HVAC & AC Problems We Repair

  • Not cooling / insufficient airflow
  • Refrigerant leaks / R134a or R410a issues
  • Electrical faults / unit won’t start
  • Strange noises / compressor or fan problems
  • Thermostat or control errors
  • Blocked drainage / water leaks
  • Air quality / filter issues
  • Duct or vent repairs
